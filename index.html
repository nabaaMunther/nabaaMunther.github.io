About Me

I am Dr. Nabaa Munther, a devoted and ambitious pediatrician based at Al-Batool Children’s Hospital in Diyala, Iraq. I am currently preparing for the final stage of the Arab Board in Pediatrics, combining strong clinical practice with academic commitment.

My professional journey is defined by purpose, resilience, and a dedication to continuous development. I specialize in presenting complex pediatric cases with clarity and precision, and I believe every challenge is an opportunity to grow and evolve.

Beyond clinical practice, I strive to leave a mark both academically and socially — through meaningful research, mentorship, and empowering the presence of women in medicine with grace and strength.

Areas of Focus

  • Neonatal care and jaundice
  • Pediatric hematology and rare blood disorders
  • Child nutrition, development, and preventive follow-up

My Vision

To be recognized not only as a skilled pediatrician, but as a symbol of elegance, intellect, and leadership in modern medicine — blending science, empathy, and ambition to shape a brighter future for children and for myself.
